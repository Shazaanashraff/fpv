📱 Phone Number Submission Test

How This Works:

  • Frontend (Shopify Form): Collects phone number, validates with OTP
  • Backend (Vercel API): Only handles OTP send/verify via Twilio
  • Shopify (Form Submit): Receives phone number when form posts

Important: The Vercel backend does NOT send data to Shopify. When you click "Create Account", the browser submits the form to Shopify directly with all field values including the phone number.
